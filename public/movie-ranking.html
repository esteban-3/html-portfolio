The Best Movies According to Esteban

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Good Will Hunting

I am no where near as intelligent as the main character, but I think we relate in many ways, especially with our background. Growing up, I was surrounded by many people who don't value education, and my high school had a small percentage of honors and AP students.

Paprika

Have you watched Inception directed by Christopher Nolan? A lot of that was inspired by this animtaed film. As a fan of anime, this animated film is a must.

Chronicle

I have watched this film over 10 times. I am a huge fan of superpower films, and the twist in this film is nothing like I have witnessed before.
